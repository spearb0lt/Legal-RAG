SOURCE_NOTE: Verbatim text of Article 21. This is one of the most-litigated articles of the Constitution; the right to privacy, right to dignity, right to a clean environment, and many other rights have been read into it through case law (e.g., K.S. Puttaswamy v. Union of India (2017) 10 SCC 1).

Article 21. Protection of life and personal liberty.

No person shall be deprived of his life or personal liberty except according to procedure established by law.
